- HOW TO PLAY -

👁️ Every player sees the secret word

Except for one player—they're the Odd One Out and don’t know the word!

❓ One player is left in the dark

They must blend in by answering questions without knowing the word.

💬 Ask & Answer

Each player asks a question to another player, who must answer honestly.

🗳️ Vote Time

At the end, vote on who you think the Odd One Out was.

🏆 Reveal & Score

The game reveals the Odd One Out and tallies scores based on correct guesses and trickery!
